Our Services

  • Environmental Education: We develop and implement educational programs, workshops, and awareness campaigns to raise awareness about environmental issues and promote sustainable living practices.
  • Awareness Campaigns: We conduct awareness campaigns to educate the public about pressing environmental issues and promote action towards achieving the United Nations Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs).
  • Workshops and Educational Events: We organize workshops, seminars, and educational events focused on environmental education, sustainable development, and conservation practices.
  • Tree Planting and Reforestation: We actively participate in tree-planting initiatives and collaborate with local communities, schools, and government agencies to restore ecosystems, combat climate change, and promote biodiversity conservation.
  • Promotion of Environmental Courses: We partner with organizations like WESSA and Common Earth to promote environmental courses that empower individuals with knowledge and skills to address environmental challenges.
  • Evaluation and Monitoring: We continuously evaluate the impact of our initiatives, measure progress towards our goals, and ensure accountability to our donors and stakeholders.
  • Advocacy and Policy: We advocate for policies and initiatives that address environmental challenges, promote renewable energy, and advance environmental justice.
  • Mining and Materials for Sustainable Development Transformation: We actively engage in initiatives related to mining and materials for sustainable development transformation.
  • Environmental Justice: We work to promote environmental justice by advocating for equitable access to clean air, water, and natural resources, and addressing systemic inequalities that perpetuate environmental harm.

Climate Guide

Knowing more about the big issues facing the Climate, Nature, Food, and Fashion gives you more power to make the right decisions for you and the future of our Generation and the planet. Humanity, Animals, Plants, and other Living beings need a specific Climate in order to live. Since the Industrial Revolution, Our Planet has been heating up at a much quicker rate than ever before-with damaging consequences. We have been burning fossil fuels such as Coal and Gas to Provide Electricity, Heating, and Transporting, but this releases CARBON DIOXIDE, Methane, and other Gases into the atmosphere. They hang in the air like an invisible blanket, trapping the heat from the Sun and stopping it from returning to space, warming up the Earth’s surface temperature. As a result, we are seeing extreme weather patterns, from Floods to Droughts across the Globe. It is also causing Glaciers and Ice Caps to melt, causing Sea levels to rise. While we cannot reverse the damage, we can make changes to slow things down, reduce our impact here on Earth, and create a more SUSTAINABLE FUTURE.
