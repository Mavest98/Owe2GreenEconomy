About Us

Welcome to Owe2 Green Economy, where our mission is to inspire environmental stewardship, promote sustainability, and advocate for environmental justice. We are dedicated to fostering a deeper understanding of environmental issues and empowering communities to take action.

Our Vision

To create a Green Carbon Caring Society where vulnerable communities are equipped with the knowledge and tools to address the climate crisis, leading the way to a sustainable future for all.

Our Mission

Our mission is to combat climate change by connecting, equipping, and amplifying the voices of communities most affected by environmental issues. We focus on creating sustainable opportunities in green jobs, promoting renewable energy, and fostering food security in township areas.

Core Values & Principles

  • Financial Sustainability: Ensuring self-sufficiency by reinvesting profits to maximize social and environmental impact.
  • Community Empowerment: Providing access to economic opportunities and strengthening the productive capacity of vulnerable communities.
  • Sustainable Practices: Fostering environmental stewardship through practical projects and educational initiatives.
  • Inclusivity & Diversity: Promoting gender equality, youth empowerment, and the inclusion of people with disabilities.
  • Circular Economy: Building sustainable economic models where resources are reused and recycled.

Business Model

Owe2 Green Economy operates as a circular economy, where resources and profits are reinvested to ensure continuous impact. The organisation generates revenue through grants, donations, fundraising events, consultancy services, and green enterprise activities.

Key Partnerships

  • Government Agencies: Collaboration for regulatory support and funding.
  • Educational Institutions: Partnering with schools to incorporate environmental education into their programs.
  • NGOs & Civil Organizations: Joint initiatives to broaden impact and share resources.
  • Businesses: Corporate partnerships for sponsorships and collaboration on sustainability initiatives.

Pillars and Sectors

  • Environmental Conservation: Reforestation, sustainable water management, biodiversity protection, and pollution control.
  • Social Empowerment: Community education, youth and women empowerment, and health and well-being.
  • Economic Sustainability: Green jobs, sustainable agriculture, circular economy practices, and renewable energy.
