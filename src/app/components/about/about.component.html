About Us

Welcome to Owe2 Green Economy, where our mission is to inspire environmental stewardship, promote sustainability, and advocate for environmental justice. As a leading environmental organization, we are dedicated to fostering a deeper understanding of environmental issues, empowering individuals and communities to take action, and advocating for policies that protect our planet and promote social equity.

Our Mission

At Owe2 Green Economy, our mission is to advance environmental education, conservation, and sustainable development practices aligned with the United Nations Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs). We strive to promote environmental protection, justice, and equity while fostering a culture of respect for nature and all living beings.

What We Do

Environmental Education: We develop and implement educational programs, workshops, and awareness campaigns to raise awareness about environmental issues and promote sustainable living practices. Through partnerships with schools, universities, and community organizations, we empower individuals of all ages to become environmental stewards and changemakers.

Awareness Campaigns: We conduct awareness campaigns to educate the public about pressing environmental issues and promote action towards achieving the United Nations Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs). Through engaging content and outreach efforts, we aim to raise awareness and inspire individuals to take meaningful steps towards sustainability.

Workshops and Educational Events: We organize workshops, seminars, and educational events focused on environmental education, sustainable development, and conservation practices. Our interactive and informative sessions cover a wide range of topics, including SDGs, inner development goals, tree planting, and environmental courses offered by partner organizations like Wildlife Environmental Society of South Africa (WESSA) and Common Earth Academy.

Tree Planting and Reforestation: We actively participate in tree-planting initiatives and collaborate with local communities, schools, and government agencies to restore ecosystems, combat climate change, and promote biodiversity conservation. Through tree planting, we aim to mitigate the effects of deforestation and contribute to a greener and healthier environment.

Promotion of Environmental Courses: We partner with organizations like WESSA and Common Earth to promote environmental courses that empower individuals with knowledge and skills to address environmental challenges. By offering scholarships and organizing outreach efforts, we strive to make environmental education accessible to all and inspire lifelong learning and environmental stewardship.

Evaluation and Monitoring: We continuously evaluate the impact of our initiatives, measure progress towards our goals, and ensure accountability to our donors and stakeholders. Through rigorous evaluation and monitoring, we strive to improve our effectiveness and maximize our positive impact on the environment and communities we serve.

Advocacy and Policy: We advocate for policies and initiatives that address environmental challenges, promote renewable energy, and advance environmental justice. By engaging with policymakers, community leaders, and grassroots organizations, we work to influence decision-making processes and drive positive change at local, national, and global levels.

Mining and Materials for Sustainable Development Transformation: As part of our commitment to sustainability, we actively engage in initiatives related to mining and materials for sustainable development transformation. This includes promoting responsible mining practices, advocating for the use of recycled and sustainable materials, and supporting initiatives that minimize the environmental impact of resource extraction and production processes.

Environmental Justice: We recognize that environmental issues disproportionately affect marginalized communities and vulnerable populations. Therefore, we work to promote environmental justice by advocating for equitable access to clean air, water, and natural resources, and addressing systemic inequalities that perpetuate environmental harm.

Our Impact

Over the years, Owe2 Green Economy has made significant strides in promoting environmental awareness, conservation, and sustainability. Through our educational programs, advocacy efforts, and community engagement initiatives, we have empowered thousands of individuals to become environmental leaders and advocates for change. Together, we are making a positive impact on our planet and creating a brighter future for generations to come.
