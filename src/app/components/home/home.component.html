Our Core Importance

Owe2 Green Economy is a forward-thinking environmental organisation dedicated to empowering local communities, conserving the environment, and promoting sustainability. Founded in January 2022, the organisation is committed to addressing Climate Change through a holistic and systemic approach that integrates education, green job creation, and environmental conservation.

Our Initiatives

Services and Programmes

We offer a wide range of services and programmes to support sustainable development, including educational workshops, community engagement projects, youth training, and enterprise support in green industries.

  • Environmental Education & Awareness
  • Community Projects
  • Youth Development Programmes
  • Enterprise Development and Green Jobs
  • Sports, Arts, Culture, & Recreation
  • Consulting & Partnerships

Business Model & Key Partnerships

Owe2 Green Economy operates on a circular economy model, where resources and profits are reinvested into the organization to sustain impact. We partner with government agencies, educational institutions, NGOs, and businesses to broaden our reach and share resources.
